How to Calculate Your Hourly Rate as a Freelancer

Whether you're a designer, developer, or VA, here's how to price your time.

Step 1: Desired Income

Start by choosing a realistic target annual income, e.g., $80,000.

Step 2: Estimate Billable Hours

Account for admin, vacation, sick days. Example: 1,400 billable hours/year.

Step 3: Add Expenses & Taxes

Include tools, insurance, taxes. e.g., $20,000/year.

Step 4: Use This Formula

Hourly Rate = (Income + Expenses) / Hours

Result: ($80,000 + $20,000) / 1400 = $71.43/hour
